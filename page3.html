Privacy Policy
1. Introduction
CAAED Corporation ("CAAED," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information collected from candidates, clients, and users of our website (collectively, "Users"). This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of personal information. By accessing or using our website or services, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
2. Information Collection
2.1 Personal Information
CAAED may collect personal information, including but not limited to:
a) Name, contact details, and email address
b) Curriculum vitae (CV) or resume content
c) Educational and professional qualifications
d) Employment history and references
e) User authentication credentials 2.2 Technical Information
When Users access our online services, our web servers automatically record visit details, including:
a) IP address
b) Access timestamps
c) Referring websites
d) Pages visited and features utilized
e) Browser type and language settings 2.3 Publicly Available Information
CAAED may collect information from public sources, such as professional networking platforms, when necessary for recruitment activities and to offer relevant career opportunities.
3. Use of Information
CAAED processes personal information for the following purposes:
a) Provision of recruitment and related services
b) Maintenance of business relationships with clients and candidates
c) Facilitation of CV submissions and job applications
d) Matching candidate profiles with job vacancies
e) Responding to inquiries
f) Direct marketing of products and services, subject to User consent
g) Fulfillment of contractual obligations with clients
h) Engagement of third-party service providers for ancillary services
4. Job Alerts
Users may subscribe to job alerts by providing their email address. This information will be used solely for the purpose of delivering relevant job listings and industry news. Users may unsubscribe from these communications at any time using the provided unsubscribe mechanism.
5. Curriculum Vitae (CV) Storage and Access
5.1 Users may submit their CV via our website or directly to our consultants for specific job applications or general consideration.
5.2 Submitted CVs will be stored in CAAED's secure database and may be accessed by CAAED recruitment consultants globally.
6. Aggregate Information
CAAED collects and analyzes aggregate information about website visitors to improve our services and user experience. This may include marketing profiling to identify services or jobs of potential interest to Users.
7. User Rights
7.1 Right of Access: Users have the right to request a copy of their personal information held by CAAED.
7.2 Right to Erasure: Users may request the deletion of their personal data, subject to certain limitations.
8. Data Retention
CAAED will retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
9. Data Security
CAAED implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
10. International Data Transfers
Personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the User's country of residence. CAAED ensures that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
11. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites. CAAED is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites.
12. Changes to this Privacy Policy
CAAED reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any material changes through our website or via email.
